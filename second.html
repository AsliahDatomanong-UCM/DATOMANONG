History of Ramadhan

From revelation to global tradition — trace the origins and milestones of this holy month.

Origins in the Qur'an

Ramadhan, the ninth month of the Islamic calendar, began in 610 CE when the Qur'an was revealed to Prophet Muhammad.

The First Revelation: Cave of Hira

During Ramadan, Prophet Muhammad retreated to the Cave of Hira where the first verses of the Qur'an were revealed.

Establishment of Fasting

In 624 CE, fasting became obligatory through Qur'an 2:183.

Key Historical Events

  • Battle of Badr (624 CE)
  • Conquest of Mecca (630 CE)
  • Ottoman & Abbasid Eras
